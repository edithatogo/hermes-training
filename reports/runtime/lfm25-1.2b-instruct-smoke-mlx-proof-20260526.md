## Decision

LFM2.5 1.2B Instruct plus the local smoke LoRA adapter is runtime-proven through
`mlx_lm.server` using SSD-local artifacts. Treat it as a low-latency local
specialist/runtime baseline only. Do not publish model-improvement claims from
this proof because the prior full-smoke evaluation showed response collapse and
the repaired direct evaluator smoke still produced a non-compliant response.
